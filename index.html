PU Campus Connect WiFi - Instructions

The process of connecting to CAMPUS CONNECT Wi-Fi is given below:
  1. Enable Wi-Fi in your Laptop/Smart phone/any other hand held device, etc.
  2. Connect your device to Campus Connect.
  3. Enter the Registered mobile number and Voucher PIN received from SAMS.
  4. OTP will be generated and sent to your registered Mobile.
  5. Enter the 4 digit OTP number in the next page.
  6. After submission, password will be sent to your registered Mobile number.
    • a. For Students - the userid will be your Register Number or Mobile Number.
    • b. For faculty/Officers/Staff - the userid will be your Employee Code
  7. Enter Userid and Password and start surf.
For any problems during the above steps from 3 to 6, kindly contact

Enrolment for MOOCs course Photojournalism by EMRC Pondicherry University

Photojournalism: Award-winning free online course on swayam.gov.in
  1. This course offers lifetime access to learning resources to photography enthusiasts to stay in touch with their craft and absorb insights from the masters such as internationally renowned Raghu Rai, Prashant Panjiar and the national photo editors of Press Trust of India (PTI) and The Hindu.
  2. In April 2023, this course received the Best MOOCs Award in the 24th CEC-UGC Educational Film Festival of India.
  3. 2640 learners in 2021 and 2584 learners in 2022 have benefitted from this MOOC. It goes live for the third time on 31st July 2023.
  4. An interested learner of any age across the world, with no prerequisites for admission, can join the course for free by clicking on the link: https://onlinecourses.swayam2.ac.in/cec23_ge21/preview
  5. An in-depth review of this course by one of the learners, Prof. Ved Kumari, Vice Chancellor of National Law University Odisha, India has been published on Class Central’s The Report publication.

  7. You can take a look at some other reviews here:
